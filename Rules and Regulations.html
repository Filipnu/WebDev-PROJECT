Thank you for considering adopting one of our cats! We’re excited to help you find your new furry friend.
Please read and agree to the following rules to ensure a safe, happy home for your new companion.

𝚁𝚞𝚕𝚎𝚜 𝚊𝚗𝚍 𝚁𝚎𝚐𝚞𝚕𝚊𝚝𝚒𝚘𝚗

  1. You must be at least 18 years old to adopt a cat. Cats are adorable, but they need responsible humans!
  2. Your adopted cat must live indoors at all times. Cats are safer, healthier, and happier when they stay inside where they belong.
  3. All adopters must provide a valid ID and proof of residence (e.g., a utility bill or lease agreement). This helps us ensure that you’re ready for your new family member.
  4. A home inspection may be required before we finalize the adoption. We just want to make sure your space is safe and cat-friendly!
  5. Your adopted cat is a lifelong companion, not a temporary guest. The cat cannot be sold, given away, or transferred to another person without notifying us. We’re here to help if anything changes
  6. If you can no longer care for your cat for any reason, please return them to us. No judgment we just want them to be safe and loved.
  7. Adopting a cat means making a lifelong commitment to their health, happiness, and well-being. Cats can live 15 more years, so be ready to provide love, care, and companionship throughout their entire life.
